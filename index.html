About Us

Background

Rootech Inc is an I.T solution provider founded by a group of I.T professionals on March 8, 2017 and registered on May 28, 2014 under the Registration of Business Names Act, 1962 ( No. 151) and issued a certificate dated May 28, 2018

Strategy & Vision

  • Purpose

    To be the leader in the Information Technology industry by providing quality I.T solution to all our client.

  • Vision

    To provide high quality Information Technology services that beats the expectations of our valued customers in Ghana and around the world.

  • Mission Statement

    To build a strong and long term relationships with all our clients and provide excellent customer services by tracking business through innovation and cutting-edge technology.

Business Activities

Rootech Inc has been established as an Information Technology solution provider at Accra in the CapitalRegion of Ghana. Our traditional business model is providing Information Technology solution and support to businesses in Africa in terms of I.T Training, Home and Enterprise Networking, Graphics Design, Online Advertisement, Web Hosting & Website Development and all IT related consult.
