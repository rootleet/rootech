Features

Core Modules

Fast & Reliable POS Billing

Process quick cash sales or complex credit transactions with speed. Features cancel bill, void items, hold bills, and process refunds automatically.

Advanced Inventory Management

The heart of your operations. Manage categories, multiple pricing levels, max/min stock alerts, and use powerful bulk operations (CSV upload, price changes, image uploads).

Inventory Movement Control

Track every item from purchase to sale. Manage Purchase Orders, Goods Receiving, Inter-Store Transfers, expiry tracking, and regular stock counts.

Back Office & Invoicing

Streamline admin tasks. Create proforma invoices, convert POS sales to formal invoices, and manage complex taxes like VAT, NHIL, GETFund seamlessly.

Comprehensive Reporting

Insights at your fingertips. Access blue (sales), gray (inventory), yellow (financial), and green (daily operations) reports to track staff performance, margins, and end-of-day balances.

EVAT Integration

Electronic VAT compliance made easy. Stay compliant with Ghana Revenue Authority (GRA) regulations with automatic VAT calculation and electronic submission.
